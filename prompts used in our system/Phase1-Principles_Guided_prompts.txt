*Aesthetic Watermark Design Principles*:

3. Functional Harmony: Maintain a balance between aesthetic integration and protective strength of the watermark.
Key Considerations:

4.Implementation Instructions:
(1) For the *Placement* of the watermark:

(3) For the *Font Size* of the watermark:
- Adjust font size to avoid excessive visual prominence.
